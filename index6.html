ICT4M 2020

ICT4M 2020 is a conference that gathers academicians, researchers, practitioners, professionals, and students to address the challenges of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) for the Muslim world.This conference has been conducted seven times so far with the most recent location in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on 23-25 July 2018. For this upcoming event, ICT4M 2020 will be conducted on 27 – 29 March 2020 at the IIUM Kuantan Campus. The theme for this year is “Embracing eHealth in Facing the Challenges of IR4.0 in the Muslim World”. It is hosted by the Kulliyyah of Information and Communication Technology, International Islamic University Malaysia (KICT, IIUM) in collaboration with CENTRIS, IIUM and will be organized in conjunction of 2020 World Congress For Integration And Islamization.


ORGANIZED BY

The International Islamic University Malaysia, also known as IIUM or UIAM, is a public publicly-funded university in Malaysia. Its main campus is located in Gombak,Selangor with its Centre for Foundation Studies situated in Petaling Jaya / Nilai and its medical-centric branch in Kuantan, Pahang.
